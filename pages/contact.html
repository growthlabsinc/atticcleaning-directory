Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find contractors in my area?

Simply enter your city or ZIP code in the search bar on our homepage or Find Services page to see available contractors near you.

Yes! Our directory is completely free for homeowners. There are no fees to search for contractors or request quotes.

We verify that contractors have proper licensing and insurance before listing them in our directory. However, we always recommend doing your own due diligence.
